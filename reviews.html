Professional standards

Peer-Review Policy

I approach peer review as a scholarly responsibility, assessing methods, evidence, interpretation, originality, clarity, and reproducibility while respecting authors and preserving the confidentiality of editorial processes.

01

Confidentiality

Manuscripts, author identities, editorial correspondence, recommendations, and other non-public materials are not disclosed.

02

Independence

Potential conflicts of interest are considered carefully before accepting or completing a review.

03

Constructive Assessment

Review comments focus on clear, respectful, and actionable improvements supported by scientific reasoning.

04

Research Integrity

Methodological soundness, evidence, transparency, reproducibility, and appropriate attribution are evaluated carefully.

Public verification without compromising confidentiality

This website displays public researcher identifiers, profile links, aggregate public peer-review information, and links to authoritative records. It does not publish passwords, API secrets, confidential manuscript titles, author information, review reports, recommendations, or private editorial correspondence.
